In this imagined universe, humans live underwater in bioluminescent cities, adapted to the ocean’s depths, while marine species have evolved to thrive on land, building habitats and roaming terrestrial landscapes. This reversal highlights themes of adaptation, coexistence, and the interconnectedness of life in contrasting environments.

  • Vision : TerraNova's purpose is to protect the earth living species keeping it's original purpose which is defending the earth & nature.
  • Mission : TerraNova’s vision is a world where our earth thrive again, teeming with life and biodiversity .
